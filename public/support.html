Before reporting a problem

  • Reload the page completely. Preview builds may be retained temporarily by the service worker or browser cache.
  • Confirm that JavaScript, local storage, WebGL, and site audio are allowed.
  • Try the Full, System, or Reduced motion setting if visual effects behave unexpectedly.
  • When an AI interpretation fails, the source text, Tree, Rites, Gematria, and local journal should remain usable.

Saved readings

Readings are stored in the current browser profile. They do not automatically follow you to another browser, device, private tab, or reinstalled application. Clearing site storage can remove them permanently.

Useful information for a report

  • Device and operating-system version.
  • Browser and browser version.
  • Whether the device was a phone, Fold closed, Fold open, tablet, desktop, or installed PWA.
  • The section being used: Oracle, Rites, Tree, Gematria, or Grimoire.
  • A screenshot with private questions or journal content obscured.
  • The exact sequence of actions that produced the problem.

Report a defect or request support

Use the project's GitHub issue tracker. Search existing issues before opening a new one. Do not post API keys, private journal entries, personal identifying information, or confidential Oracle questions.

Urgent situations

This application does not provide emergency, medical, mental-health, legal, or crisis support. Contact an appropriate local service or qualified professional when immediate assistance is needed.

Include enough reproducible detail to distinguish a content question, browser limitation, AI-provider error, and application defect.
