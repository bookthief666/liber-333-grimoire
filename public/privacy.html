Privacy Policy

Liber 333 is designed primarily as a local study and journaling tool. The AI-assisted Oracle is the principal feature that may transmit information beyond your device.

Information stored on your device

The application may store saved readings, questions, chapter selections, recurrence counts, presentation preferences, and related ritual context in your browser's local storage. This information is not synchronized to an account because the current application does not provide user accounts.

You can remove locally stored information through the Grimoire controls or by clearing this site's storage in your browser or device settings. Clearing site data may permanently remove saved readings.

AI-assisted Oracle requests

When you request an AI interpretation, the application may send your question and relevant reading context to the application's server endpoint and then to the configured external AI provider. Context may include selected chapters, calculated correspondences, timing information, and a limited summary of recent readings when continuity features are enabled.

Do not enter information you consider highly sensitive, confidential, or identifying into an Oracle question. External AI, hosting, security, and network providers may process operational data under their own policies.

Operational and technical information

The application does not intentionally create an advertising profile or sell personal information. Hosting and infrastructure providers may nevertheless create ordinary operational logs, such as request time, network address, browser information, error details, rate-limit events, and security signals. These logs may be used to operate, secure, diagnose, and control the cost of the service.

Permissions and device capabilities

The web application may use optional browser capabilities such as vibration, speech synthesis, audio playback, installation prompts, local file download, and local storage. These capabilities are used only to provide the corresponding application feature. Availability varies by browser and device.

Children

The application is not directed to children under 13. The source text and commentary may contain mature philosophical, religious, sexual, or ritual themes.

Security and retention

No online service can guarantee absolute security. Local journal information remains until you remove it or the browser removes site data. Operational data handled by hosting or AI providers may be retained according to their service configuration and policies.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the application gains accounts, synchronization, analytics, native-device functions, or different AI providers. The effective date above will change when material revisions are published.

Contact

Questions, privacy requests, and security reports can be submitted through the project's public support tracker. Avoid posting private information in a public issue.

Do what thou wilt shall be the whole of the Law. Use the application with deliberate attention to what you choose to record or transmit.
